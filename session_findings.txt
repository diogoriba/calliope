Turns out the sessionID that you get from the cookie while authorizing a socket is slightly different than the one you get from request.sessionID.

Example: if your request.sessionID is +PtdkFnzth1QxpYdz3mTiOvl, it would be somthing like this in the cookie:
s:+PtdkFnzth1QxpYdz3mTiOvl.iuVorv14NNbhsXTE32yTp7TB3B1jICFcv8kxS8xl/FE

I still haven't figured out why, but it looks like the sessionID can be captured with /^s:(?<sessionID>[^\.])+\./.
